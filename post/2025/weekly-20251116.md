## Highlights
### Playing with LaBRADOR: Building Compact Lattice-based Proofs with Recursion
LaBRADOR is a lattice-based proof system that uses recursion and Ajtai commitments to achieve sublinear proof sizes, offering a post-quantum alternative to hash-based SNARKs.

LaBRADOR 是一种基于格的递归证明系统，结合 Ajtai 承诺实现子线性证明大小，为后量子时代提供了 SNARK 替代方案。
- <https://blog.zksecurity.xyz/posts/labrador/>
### Proximity Gaps: What Happened and How Does It Affect our SNARKs
New research disproves parts of the proximity gaps conjecture, impacting SNARK parameter choices. Projects must now trade off between proven security with higher costs or continue using unproven but efficient settings.


最新研究推翻了部分接近性间隙猜想，影响了 SNARK 参数设定。项目需在更高安全性与更优性能之间做出权衡。
- <https://blog.zksecurity.xyz/posts/proximity-conjecture/>
### The Trustless Manifesto
The Trustless Manifesto calls on Ethereum builders to preserve trustlessness — systems where correctness and fairness rely on math and consensus, not intermediaries. It warns against the creeping centralization caused by convenience and urges developers to prioritize openness, verifiability, and user sovereignty, even at the cost of complexity.


「去信任宣言」呼吁以太坊建设者坚持去信任性，构建依赖数学与共识而非中介的系统。文章警示便利性带来的中心化倾向，强调应优先保障开放性、可验证性与用户主权，即使这意味着更高的复杂度与成本。
- <https://trustlessness.eth.limo/general/2025/11/11/the-trustless-manifesto.html>
### To Have Machines Make Math Proofs, Turn Them Into a Puzzle
Computer scientist Marijn Heule uses SAT solvers to transform complex math problems into logic puzzles solvable by machines. By combining SAT with large language models (LLMs), he envisions a future where AI can help prove theorems beyond human reach. He argues that trust in automated reasoning may be more valuable than human understanding, and sees collaboration between humans and AI as the key to advancing mathematics.


计算机科学家 Marijn Heule 利用 SAT 求解器将复杂数学问题转化为逻辑谜题，并借助大语言模型（LLMs）推动 AI 解出人类无法解决的定理。他认为在数学中「信任」比「理解」更重要，未来应由人类与 AI 协作，共同推动数学研究的突破。
- <https://www.quantamagazine.org/to-have-machines-make-math-proofs-turn-them-into-a-puzzle-20251110/>
### The Quest for Practical iO with Machina iO
In this episode, Anna Rose and Tarun Chitra chat with Sora Suegami and Enrico Bottazzi from Machina iO. They explain indistinguishability obfuscation (iO) technology and how they are working to bring this powerful cryptographic primitive from theoretical territory into the practical world. They discuss how the pair got into iO and how new assumptions like all-product LWE and evasive LWE will help bridge theory to practice.

They explore the benchmarks, the challenges and opportunities of this cutting-edge privacy cryptography and cover potential optimizations and real-world uses. While iO is still far from being truly practical, their work shows tangible steps ahead and offers interesting insights into how this could actually work.

在本期节目中，Anna Rose 和 Tarun Chitra 与 Machina iO 的 Sora Suegami 和 Enrico Bottazzi 进行了对话。他们解释了不可区分混淆 (iO) 技术，以及他们如何致力于将这一强大的密码学原语从理论领域带入实际应用。他们探讨了两人是如何涉足 iO 领域的，以及诸如全乘积 LWE 和规避型 LWE 等新假设将如何帮助理论与实践相连接。

他们探讨了这项前沿隐私密码学的基准测试、挑战和机遇，并介绍了潜在的优化方案和实际应用。虽然 iO 距离真正实用化还有很长的路要走，但他们的工作展现了切实可行的进步，并为我们深入了解其运作方式提供了有趣的视角。
- <https://zeroknowledge.fm/podcast/384/>
### Announcing the S-two Book 
ZKSecurity and Starkware have released the S-two Book, a comprehensive guide to using Starkware’s next-gen S-two prover. It covers writing AIRs, implementing Cairo AIR, and understanding Circle STARKs, offering valuable resources for developers exploring high-performance zero-knowledge proofs.


ZKSecurity 与 Starkware 合作发布了 S-two Book，系统介绍了新一代 S-two 证明器的使用方法，包括编写 AIR、实现 Cairo AIR 以及理解 Circle STARKs，适合开发者深入学习高性能零知识证明技术。
- <https://blog.zksecurity.xyz/posts/stwo-book/>
### Trusted Execution Environments, w/ Andrew Miller 
In this module, Guillermo Angeris and Andrew Miller, Associate Professor at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign and Junior Product Manager at Teleport Studio, provide a comprehensive overview of Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), explaining their core principles of hardware-enforced isolation and remote attestation. Andrew covers the trust model involving manufacturers and hosting providers, the architectural differences between process-based (SGX) and VM-based (TDX) TEEs, and gives a detailed walkthrough of using a TEE as a blockchain coprocessor for a private auction. The module concludes by addressing critical security considerations and pitfalls, including side-channel attacks, replay attacks, and the complex challenge of managing persistent state and secure software upgrades.

本模块由 Guillermo Angeris 与 Andrew Miller 教授深入解析可信执行环境（TEE），涵盖其硬件强制隔离、远程认证核心原理，以及 SGX 与 TDX 的架构差异。通过私有拍卖案例演示 TEE 作区块链协处理器的实际应用，并剖析侧信道攻击、持久状态管理等关键安全挑战。
- <https://zkhack.dev/whiteboard/s3-m6/>
### How we avoided side-channels in our new post-quantum Go cryptography libraries
Trail of Bits released constant-time Go implementations of post-quantum signature schemes ML-DSA and SLH-DSA. To prevent side-channel attacks like KyberSlash, they eliminated branches and divisions using techniques such as conditional swaps and Barrett reduction, ensuring secure and efficient cryptographic operations.


Trail of Bits 发布了后量子签名算法 ML-DSA 和 SLH-DSA 的 Go 实现，并通过条件交换、Barrett 降维等技术消除分支与除法操作，有效防止 KyberSlash 等侧信道攻击，提升密码操作的安全性与效率。
- <https://blog.trailofbits.com/2025/11/14/how-we-avoided-side-channels-in-our-new-post-quantum-go-cryptography-libraries/>
